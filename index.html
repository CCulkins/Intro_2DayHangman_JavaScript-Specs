Welcome to hang man!

Read through the directions and press the New Game button to begin!

    Rules:
  1. Pick a letter from the drop down to see if it matches a letter in the puzzle!
  2. Every time you pick a letter that is not a part of the puzzle, one of your hangman's body parts will show.
  3. If you guess the puzzle correctly, before your full hangman is displayed, you win!
  4. However, when your full hangman is displayed (after 6 missed letters) you lose.
